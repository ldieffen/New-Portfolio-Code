For class project I designed a mock magazine layout featuring a cover, table of contents, feature spread, and a department. I chose to make the magazine focus around cooking, home decor, d.i.y projects, and inspirations for your everyday life. My favorite aspects of creating this design is the hand-lettering element for the cover page and making the recipe that is shown in the photograph on the feature spread.
